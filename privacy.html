Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Local Knowledge collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our fishing forecast service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you sign up for Local Knowledge:

  • Email address (required for account and forecast delivery)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Phone number (optional, for SMS alerts)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Deliver daily fishing forecasts to your email
  • Send SMS alerts for exceptional fishing conditions (if opted in)
  • Process subscription payments
  • Communicate service updates or changes

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Stripe — for secure payment processing
  • Beehiiv — for email delivery
  • Twilio — for SMS delivery (if opted in)

These third parties are bound by their own privacy policies and data protection agreements.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS
  • Payment information never touches our servers (handled entirely by Stripe)
  • Regular security reviews of our service providers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of SMS alerts at any time
  • Cancel your subscription

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@localknowledge.fish.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts: Data retained while subscribed
  • Cancelled accounts: Data deleted within 30 days
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years per tax requirements

Cookies

Our landing page uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
privacy@localknowledge.fish
